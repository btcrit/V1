We're a club at RIT.

Meetings are on Tuesday evenings. We'll discuss assorted things, go on field trips to a cafe, and every once in a while have a guest speaker.

We Like Cryptocurrencies.

From Bitcoin to Destler Dubloons, we'll discuss it all. Big blocks vs Off-Chain Transactions? Traditional blockchains or DAG-based? You decide.

We also like decentralized systems in general. IPFS is the next internet and I2P is the next internet. TOR is the next internet, and Ethereum with be the next internet.
