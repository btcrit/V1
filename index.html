We're a club at RIT.

Meetings are on Tuesday evenings. We'll discuss assorted things, go on field trips to a cafe, and every once in a while have a guest speaker.

Hack Nights are on Fridays. We'll drag out our laptops to build websites (like this one), have trade-bot competitions, and other general activites.

We Like Cryptocurrencies.

From Bitcoin to Destler Dubloons, we'll discuss it all. Big blocks vs Off-Chain Transactions? Traditional blockchains or DAG-based? You decide.

We also like decentralized systems in general. IPFS is the next internet and I2P is the next internet. TOR is the next internet, and Ethereum with be the next internet.

Can we have your email?

We'll tell you weekly what the topic of our next meeting is, as well as details on club events and crypto news.

Just hop over into our Matrix channel. Ask whatever questions you'd like, and we'll let you know where to go from there.

The Blockchain Scene in Rochester

Krudco Skate Shop is run by some very friendly people. Their online store accepts Bitcoin, but that doesn't mean you shouldn't check them out in person.

Pop Roc Cafe has "Caffiene, Comics, and Culture". They also accept BTC and ETH, and are a generally awesome place to hang out.
